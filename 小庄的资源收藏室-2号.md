## 1. maven镜像加速配置：
```xml
<mirror>
    <id>aliyunmaven</id>
    <mirrorOf>*</mirrorOf>
    <name>阿里云公共仓库</name>
    <url>https://maven.aliyun.com/repository/public</url>
</mirror>
```
镜像官网：[点击查看](https://developer.aliyun.com/mirror/?serviceType=&tag=&keyword=maven)
